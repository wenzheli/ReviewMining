OMGosh!!! They just don't make business like this anymore (unfortunately). I just went here a couple days ago for the first time. It's a small family owned business which I love. They really put their heart and soul into this food and the proof is in the TASTE!! I had the Lechon sandwich a pork sandwich with plantains AWESOME!! You must try this place or your seriously missing out.
